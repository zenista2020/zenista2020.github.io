About Zenista TechFest '20

Zenista is a nationwide technical fest hosted by the department of Electronics and Communication Engineering of Sri Sairam Engineering College. Every year, Zenista draws students from various colleges whose minds are challenged with new real world problems, helping them become better engineers. This year marks the 23rd time we are hosting this fest and we have included some new, exciting events. Come on- Letâ€™s create, develop and innovate!

Model or Simulation
Got a project idea that you also managed to practically implement? Then weâ€™ll give you a stage to share your projects with us.

Rules:
1. Each team should consist of a MINIMUM OF 2 and a MAXIMUM OF 3 members.

2. The teams should submit an abstract of their project BY 12:00 PM OF 05/10/2020. Late submissions will not be considered.

3. These abstracts will be evaluated by our team of experts and the list of selected teams will be announced on 06/10/2020 by evening through Instagram and Facebook. The teams will also be intimated through email.

4. Each team will get a total of 15 minutes on stage- 5 minutes for presenting, 5 minutes for demonstration (working model or simulation) and a 5 minute question-answer session.

5. The winning team and the first two runner up teams will receive certificates and cash awards.

6. All the shortlisted teams will receive participation certificates.

Note:
Time clashes with any other event in this TechFest should be reported to the event coordinator BY 07/10/2020 so that necessary arrangements can be made. Participants who fail to do so will have to manage these clashes on their own and the organisers will not be held responsible.
Idea Submission
Got an idea for doing a project? Why wait? There is no better time than now to present your ideas on our platform.

Rules:
1. Form a team consisting of a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 3 members.

2. Submit your abstract by 05/10/2020 (12PM). Late submissions will not be considered.

3. The selected teams will be notified via our Facebook and Instagram page and also through emails by the evening of 6th October (06/10/2020).

4. You get to present your ideas for 15 minutes - 10 minutes for presenting and 5 minutes for answering the queries (Q and A session).

5. The top 3 teams will receive cash awards along with certificates and all shortlisted teams will receive participation certificates.

Note:
If your team/team members are participating in any other events in this TechFest at the same time, please make sure that you have notified our event coordinator to make the necessary arrangements. If you fail to do so the coordinators will not be held responsible.
MindLab Einstein
It is a technical quiz competition.

Rules:
1. The Event will be conducted online on Kahoot!

2. The quiz will be conducted on 08/10/2020 for 1 hour.

3. Never used Kahoot! before? Worry not - Our organizers will guide you on how to use Kahoot! before the start of event.

4. Please ensure that you join 10 minutes before the event starts. Joining late will cause a commotion for the first time Kahoot! users.

5. Answer the questions and the winners will be displayed on screen by Kahoot!

6. The top 3 will be considered as winners.

7. All participants will receive participation certificates.

The Final Problem
It is an event where the teams are given a real world problem statement for which they have to come up with a solution of reasonable practicality.

Rules:
1. Participants should register as a team of two. Teams with members from different colleges should mention the same.

2. The event will take place for one hour, where the team will be given a real world situation which they have to come up with a practical solution for.

3. Teams are requested to be prepared with the basic necessary stationery items well in advance (pens, pencils, rulers, geometry boxes etc.)

4. On the day of the event, the teams can discuss with one another through any means apart from the meeting platform. Teams are requested to ensure that they have set up the required platform in advance.

5. Teams are requested to ensure that they have a stable internet connection so that submissions are done within the deadlines.

6. Winners will be awarded with prizes and certificates. All participants will receive participation certificates.

4. Winners will be awarded with certificates.
